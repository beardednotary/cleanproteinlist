How We Ranked the Best Protein Powders

Our rankings are based on verified safety testing from independent third parties, not marketing claims or brand promises.

Our Ranking Criteria:

1. Safety (70% of score)

  • Consumer Reports testing - October 2025 heavy metal analysis (lead, arsenic, cadmium, mercury)
  • Clean Label Project certification - "Clean Sixteen" non-detect status
  • NSF Certified for Sport - Batch testing for contaminants
  • Informed Choice/Sport - Third-party quality verification

Why safety is 70% of the score: Heavy metals bioaccumulate in your body. Daily exposure to contaminated protein can cause neurological damage, organ damage, and developmental issues over time.

2. Value (20% of score)

  • Cost per serving ($/serving)
  • Protein per dollar ($/ 25g protein)
  • Availability (Amazon, major retailers)

3. Performance (10% of score)

  • Protein content per serving
  • Taste & mixability (verified user reviews)
  • Ingredient quality
