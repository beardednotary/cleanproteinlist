Protein Source Average Contamination Top Rankings

Why whey is cleaner:

  • Derived from milk - cows act as biological filters, don't absorb heavy metals like plants
  • Single dairy source vs multiple contaminated plant sources
  • Highly processed and filtered - removes impurities
  • No soil contamination vector (plants absorb lead from agricultural soil)

2. Multi-Source Protein Blend Strategy

BSN Syntha-6 uses a proprietary blend of 6 protein sources:

  • Milk protein isolate
  • Egg albumin

Why this matters for safety: All sources are animal-derived (whey, casein, egg), which means zero plant-based contamination risk. The blend strategy actually reduces risk by diversifying sources within the clean dairy/egg category.

3. Established Manufacturing Standards

BSN (Bio-Engineered Supplements and Nutrition) has been manufacturing supplements since 2001. Benefits of an established manufacturer:

  • cGMP (current Good Manufacturing Practices) certified facilities
