What Makes Dymatize ISO-100 Safe?

1. Whey Protein Isolate (Not Plant-Based)

The #1 factor determining protein powder safety is the protein source.

Why Whey Isolate Is Inherently Safer:

  • Derived from milk: Cows don't pass heavy metals into milk at significant levels
  • Ultra-filtered: Whey isolate undergoes additional filtration removing contaminants
  • 99% pure protein: Higher purity = less potential for contamination

Plant proteins absorb lead from soil during growth. Pea protein (used in Naked Nutrition, Huel, etc.) consistently tests 10-15x higher for lead contamination.

All 4 worst-ranked products (#20-23) were plant-based. All top 6 safest products were whey-based.

2. Hydrolyzed for Purity

Dymatize ISO-100 uses hydrolyzed whey protein isolate:

  • Additional processing step removes more impurities
  • Faster absorption AND cleaner product

3. Quality Control Standards

Dymatize (owned by Glanbia) follows strict manufacturing protocols:

  • FDA-registered facilities
  • GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) certified
  • Third-party testing for contaminants
  • Batch testing for heavy metals

4. Premium Ingredient Sourcing

Dymatize sources whey protein primarily from:

  • US dairy farms (strict agricultural standards)

  • Suppliers with documented heavy metal testing
