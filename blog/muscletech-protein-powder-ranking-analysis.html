Why Is MuscleTech So Much Safer Than Competitors?

1. Whey-Based Formula (Not Plant-Based)

The #1 factor in protein powder safety is the protein source.

MuscleTech uses whey protein derived from cow's milk. Cows don't pass heavy metals into milk at significant levels, making whey inherently cleaner than plant proteins.

‚ö†Ô∏è Plant Protein Contamination Problem

Plant proteins (pea, hemp, rice) absorb heavy metals from agricultural soil:

  • Pea protein: 10-15x more lead than whey on average
  • Rice protein: High arsenic risk
  • Hemp protein: Variable lead levels

The worst products tested were ALL plant-based:

  • #23: Naked Nutrition (pea-based): 7.86 ¬µg lead

MuscleTech's decision to focus on whey is a significant safety advantage.
