Why Does Naked Nutrition Have So Much Lead?

Three factors combine to create this extreme contamination:

1. Plant-Based Protein (Primary Source)

Naked Nutrition uses pea + rice protein blend.

Plant proteins absorb lead from soil through their roots. Plants cannot distinguish between beneficial minerals and toxic metals, so they absorb both.

Result: Plant proteins have 10-15x more lead than whey proteins. Consumer Reports found 77% of plant proteins exceeded safe limits vs only 28% of whey proteins.

2. Massive Serving Size (Amplifies Problem)

Naked Nutrition Vegan Mass Gainer uses 170g serving size (compared to typical 30-35g protein servings).

Larger serving = more total protein powder = more accumulated contamination from that powder.

Even if the contamination per gram were "moderate," the huge serving size concentrates it into dangerous territory.

3. Chocolate Flavor (Adds Cadmium)

The tested product was chocolate flavor.

Chocolate flavoring comes from cocoa beans that naturally accumulate cadmium from tropical volcanic soils. While Consumer Reports primarily reported lead levels, chocolate proteins typically have 110x more cadmium than vanilla.

Naked Nutrition combines plant protein lead + chocolate cadmium = double contamination.
