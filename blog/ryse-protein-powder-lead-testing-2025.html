What RYSE Actually Claims

Critical problems with this claim:

  • No specifics: "Quality and purity" doesn't confirm heavy metal testing
  • Internal testing only: Not verified by independent third party
  • No public results: No Certificates of Analysis (COAs) available
  • No detection limits specified: Testing at 10 ppm vs 0.1 ppm produces very different results

üö® Why Internal Testing Isn't Enough

The conflict of interest problem: When brands test their own products, they control:

  • Which batches get tested (best-case samples vs production average)
  • Detection limits (loose limits miss contamination)
  • Whether results are published (failures can be hidden)

Independent third-party testing eliminates this conflict. NSF, Clean Label, and Consumer Reports have no financial incentive to pass contaminated products.
