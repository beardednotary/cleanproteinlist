Why We Built This

After Consumer Reports published their protein powder contamination study on October 14, 2025, thousands of people started searching for answers about their specific brands. We built this tool to help you quickly find safe alternatives based on the latest testing data.

Our recommendations are based on:

  • Consumer Reports October 2025 and January 2026 heavy metal testing
  • Third-party certifications (NSF Certified for Sport, Informed Choice, USP Verified)
  • Manufacturer transparency about testing practices
  • Independent lab results when available
